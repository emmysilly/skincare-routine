✨ My Skincare Routine

Cleanser

Papaya + Vitamin C Superfruit Cleanser

My go-to first cleanser. I really like this one—it's been helping me fade my dark spots. The combination of papaya enzymes and lactic acid gives a gentle exfoliation while brightening.

Treatment Cleanser

Benzoyl Peroxide 5% Topical Wash

My second cleanser that I leave on for 5 minutes so the medication can absorb into my skin. It's been helpful—my face always feels clean and soft after. The downside is it sometimes makes my face red, so I skip it on days when my skin is sensitive.

Prescription Treatment

Clindamycin Phosphate Swabs

Part of my acne treatment routine. I use this as directed by my dermatologist for targeted acne management. Sometimes I skip this depending on my skin's condition for the day.

Retinoid

Tretinoin Cream USP 0.025%

My prescription retinoid—what I call my "swipes." I sometimes skip this one or just use it lightly depending on how my skin is feeling. Retinoids are powerful, so it's all about finding the right balance to avoid irritation.

Barrier Serum

10/10 Barrier Boost

A friend recommendation that I love. This is my barrier-strengthening serum with HA and peptides. I always put it under my makeup when I wear it, and I've noticed fewer breakouts since I started using it consistently.

Moisturizer

Adaptogen Deep Moisture Cream

A rich moisturizer from Youth to the People with adaptogens, peptides, and lipids. This provides the hydration and nourishment my skin needs after all the active treatments, helping restore my moisture barrier.

Serum

Superfood Skin Drip Serum

A smooth + glow serum with kale and peptides. A friend just recommended this to me, so I haven't used it as much recently, so I can't say too much about it yet. Excited to see how it works into my routine!
